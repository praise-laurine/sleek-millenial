ABOUT US

Traditions are forged by stories that are passed down from generation to generation, most of them have died off by now since the practice is not as common as before. Sleek millennial is a platform for the youth by the youth to learn about our diverse cultures securing our traditions and keeping them alive, as well as openimg up opportunities for youth development. The founders of the platform had a vision of creating a pool of resources that can be used to advocate for the youth as well as show case the talents and skills creating a job market for the young people and ensuring they have fun while MAKING IT!
